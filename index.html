About us

My name is Jess Smyth. I founded Skill Share in 2017 after struggling to find work. I didn't think twice about stepping away from my career whilst I raised a family. I definitely didn't consider whether it would affect future job prospects.

I found a temporary job when my when my daughter started pre-school. I didn't find another for over a year. I wondered how many others were still stuck?

I wanted to help - so I Started Skill Share, volunteering my spare time when I could. It is now my full-time job with several support staff. Skill Share is a free service for anyone who has gaps in their employment and is struggling to find work.

Services

Mentor Pairing

We pair you with someone who acts as a guide; creating a plan of action and your 'go-to' for any advice.

Community

place to make friends! Members offer their time to help train others and share knowledge about upcoming positions. A way to get a foot in the door. Its a positive environment of reciprocity

Careers Events

We have guest speakers, recruiters, motivational speakers and sucess stories from people who have benefited from being a member in the past
